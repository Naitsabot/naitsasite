# I Loooove Fried Chicken

This recipe is very customisable, as it is extremely easy to make.  
Any of the spices can be changed to taste.

## Ingredients

Servings: Two maybe three, I don't know.

- 700 g chicken (e.g., boneless thigh is a good one)  
- Panko bread crumbs  
- Olive oil  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 2 tsp ground pepper  
- Paprika powder (as much as you'd like; smoked or spicy or both)  
- Dried oregano (as you like)  
- Dried coriander leaves (as you like)  

## Preparation

- Heat up the oven to 200°C  
- Get a 4 L bag, or something else  
- Put the chicken in the bag, with all spices  
- Tumble the bag a lot so that everything is coated  
- Add a fair amount of olive oil and tumble until it seems evenly coated on the chicken. If necessary, add more  
- Add a fair amount of panko and tumble. You want it to coat everything so that it seems reasonably dry. Add more if necessary  
- Empty the bag onto an oven tray with a baking sheet. Spread evenly and flat  
- Put into the oven for 25–30 minutes  
- Take out and let cool a bit before feasting on your glorious fried chicken
